## Notes

**Product Hunt specific:**
- Tagline matters a lot - it's what people see in the feed
- First 3 lines are critical - most people don't expand
- Gallery images should be high quality (no blurry screenshots)
- Maker comment should be personal and authentic

**Hacker News specific:**
- Technical depth appreciated
- Show HN gets more engagement than regular posts
- Be ready for skeptical questions about "just a wrapper"
- Demonstrate you've thought about safety/security

**General:**
- Launch day is only the start - sustained engagement matters more
- Quality of early comments matters more than raw upvote count
- Authenticity beats polish for developer tools
